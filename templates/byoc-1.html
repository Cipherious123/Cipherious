Build Your Own Cipher

Your password must meet the following criteria:

  • It must be exactly 95 characters long.
  • Ensure that each character of the password is unique
  • It must contain each of these characters exactly once:
    ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZabcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz0123456789+/!"#$%&'()*,-.:;<=>?@[\\]^_`{|}~
    Space (use the spacebar for this).

The first character in your password will replace all 'A' characters in the plaintext, the second replaces 'B', and so on up to the space character.
